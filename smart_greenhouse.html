Smart greenhouse

Our Smart Paddock integrates smart ear tags to monitor the health of livestock. To meet our customer needs of a low cost ear tag that could be deployed in the thousands over large farm properties,our smart ear tags came out on top of all other technologies for its long distance capabilities. Smart Paddock deploys GPS tracking for cattle with the use of our smart ear tags designed to survive the harshest environmental and operational conditions. You can track their livestock in real-time through your mobile phone or computer, get text alerts for stray/stolen or downed animals, review pasture usage maps, monitor the activity level of key animals and see how far the animals are walking each day to access food or water. Our solution is being positioned as an ideal network solution as the tag can support and encourage addition other operational Internet of Things (IoT) sensors to your farms to monitor water level, set up electric fences that utilise an existing radio network, among others.
