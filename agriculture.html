Intervention or a proportional control mechanism,

Traditional greenhouses control the environmental parameters through manual intervention or a proportional control mechanism, which often results in production loss, energy loss, and increased labor costs. IoT-driven smart greenhouses can intelligently monitor as well as control the climate, eliminating the need for manual intervention.

Animal theft, illness and activity

Large farm owners can use wireless IoT applications to monitor the location, well-being, and health of their cattle,goats. With this information, they can identify sick animals, so that they can be separated from the herd to prevent the spread of disease.
