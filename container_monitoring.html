Cargo Monitoring / Tracking

In recent years, not only have safety, security and transparency become significantly more important, but concerns about efficient container handling and the risks associated with the flow of information from cargo and container tracking have also increased.

Monitor the location, condition, and security due to cargo and container tracking of shipments in real-time and react proactively to disruptions Intercontinental cargo and container shipments often require extensive monitoring. Real-time cargo monitoring reveals what’s happening to the cargo while in transit in between logistics milestones. Tracking the location and monitoring the condition of cargo verifies the quality of incoming components and monitors the fulfillment of safety and environmental regulations.

  • Track and monitor all your shipments over air, land, and sea
  • View all cargo movements in real-time on an interactive map
  • Predict ETA dynamically based on shipment location
  • Digitalize processes, improve operations and decrease admin costs
  • Reveal cargo mishandling hotspots
  • Respond to disruptions in real-time
